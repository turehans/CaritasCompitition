How can we Help

In this modern-day science has proved that climate change is an extreme concern. And we shouldn’t be worried about whether humans caused climate change but how we can stop it.

But what can we actually do to help?

How you can help

The number one goal we can have is to help bring down and limit the use of fossil fuels and replace them with renewable and cleaner sources of energy. One Swedish Professor said that “We need to cut CO2 emissions almost in half by the end of the next decade.”

To help humans can do as little as not driving as often, to only paying for energy from service electricity providers who don’t burn fossil fuels.
You can also:
  Buy more green energy.
  Eat a plant based diet. 
  Replace your gasoline with hybrid.
  Wash your clothes with cold water.
  Recycle. 
  Hang Dry clothes. 
  Upgrade light bulbs.
