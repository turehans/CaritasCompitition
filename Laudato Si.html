What is Laudato Si’

In Simple Terms the Laudato Si’ is a worldwide wake up call to help stop climate change. It addresses the problem of Climate Change and at the same time has many philosophical, theological, and cultural causes that threaten the relationships of "man to nature" as said in the letter

This document is one of the greatest works of Pope Francis. Although it is an unexpected topic for the Church it is extremely relivant in modern times. And unlike most 'papal documents' Pope Francis adresses this document to all people.

The are six parts to the Laudato Si’.
Part 1: What is happening to our common home.
Part 2: The Gospel of Creation.
Part 3: The human roots of the Ecological Crisis.
Part 4: Integral Ecology.
Part 5: Lines of approach and action.
Part 6: Ecological Education and Spirituality.
